When eventually the ovens were fixed at the new shop, the tremendously hard work 
was if anything intensified. George used to mix 100 stone of bread in 12 hours, and 
Kate served in the shop, which was open from 8 o'clock in the morning to 12 o'clock 
at night. At that time pastries and buns were sold at 32 pieces for one shilling.
